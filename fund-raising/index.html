Fund raising

Fun at training!

Your District Needs You!

The representative teams of Orpington & District PSFA receive no external funding and rely entirely on the support of parents and friends at our various fundraising events such as the Shoot Out, Curry Night and Race Night to fund our trial system, indoor coaching, minor kit replacement and Jersey Tour extras.

Donations, fundraising ideas, offers of sponsorship are always welcome; as an association we do not levy match fees as we believe players should not have to pay to represent their town, for extra information contact mail@obpsfa.com.

An Easy Way To Raise Funds

Do you shop online? Did you know that every time you buy something you could be raising money for Orpington & District PSFA?

That’s right – over 2400 well known retailers including Amazon, M&S, Boden, Waitrose, House of Fraser, Vodafone, Virgin Atlantic and many more, will donate a percentage of what you spend to Orpington & District PSFA! when you shop with them, via fundraising website easyfundraising.org.uk

An Easy Way To Raise Funds

It’s like nectar but instead of earning points, each purchase generates a donation. So instead of going directly to a retailer’s website, go to easyfundraising first and choose the retailer you want to shop with – then everything you spend with that retailer earns a free donation for Orpington & District PSFA. It’s completely free to use and your shopping won’t cost you a penny more.

On average, each retailer will donate 5% of the cost of your shopping – and those donations soon mount up.

So whatever you need to buy, from your weekly grocery shop or fashion must-have, to your business travel, office supplies or mobile phone, buy it via easyfundraising and raise money for Orpington & District PSFA at no extra cost to you!

Please register to supporting us when you shop online: http://www.easyfundraising.org.uk/causes/opsfat
