Your District Needs You!

The representative teams of Orpington & District PSFA receive no external funding and rely entirely on the support of parents and friends at our various fundraising events such as the Shoot Out, Curry Night and Race Night to fund our trial system, indoor coaching, minor kit replacement and Jersey Tour extras.

Donations, fundraising ideas, offers of sponsorship are always welcome; as an association we do not levy match fees as we believe players should not have to pay to represent their town, for extra information contact orpingtonpsfau11@virginmedia.com .

The Association also acknowledges the generous sponsorship from, Paul Cantara, International Entertainer and Sidcup Hill Dental Care.
