Sun Apr 09 2017, by Joe, Charlie, and Harry

the moment we are writing this, we are about to go down for dinner. Roast Beef, but we are going to start off by telling you our overall result of our match against the confident Gloucester.

This morning the weather brilliant and it was very hot, as we kicked off. We were the dominate side creating many more chances than the Gloucester. By the half time there were no goals scored.

However we had come closer to goal but we could not finish it into until Alex finished the ball into the back of the net.

During the final few minutes, Gloucester were realy pushing for a goal but we held them off and the final whistle went.

After the match we came back to the hotel for a well deserved fish and chip lunch. In the afternoon we played on the beach at St Brelades bay to relax and have some fun. We wont say what Charlie did !!!!!!!!!
