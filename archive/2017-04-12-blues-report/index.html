Blues report

Wed Apr 12 2017, by Will and Jimi

⟵ Back to archive

Today was marvellous,at the start of the day we had breakfast and like always it was lovely.After our delicious breakfast we went to our match at the F.B playing fields.Today the blues went up against St Albans and the reds went up against Barking and Dagenham.The blues didnt get off to the greatest of starts but of course we never gave up and in the second half we got our reward.For our first equaliser the keeper made a good save and Teddy was there for the rebound.Teddy hit a amazing strike to make it 1-1 from 15 yards.15 minutes later out of pure unluckyness,Archie scored a great goal,an OWN GOAL!With only a few seconds left Mark played a great through ball right into the path of Will who took his chance and put it into the back of the net.St Albans took the kick-off and that was the game.

After the game,we went to the sea and climed lots of rocks and we saw some of the German bunkers from World War 2.On the way back to the minibus Mark had a big fall and fell into a pool of water and got absolutely soaked.When we got back to the hotel the blues went into the hotel swimming pool and had lots of fun.When we got back into our rooms we got changed into our suits and went down for our dinner.The dinner was definately top class.
