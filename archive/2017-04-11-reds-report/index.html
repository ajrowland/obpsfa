After a few minutes, Potters Bar were on the counter attack immediately and with a few threatening attacks Potters bar scored a fantastic goal followed by 2 more which lead us trailing 4-1 with 8 minutes to go and the team still kept hope and carried on attacking. With 2 perfect crosses by Jonny (Editor: Sonny?), Joe scored 2 fantastic headers. In the last 30 seconds Alex won a penalty which lead the Potters Bar mangers furious. Alfie despatched the ball bottom left corner with the last kick of the game which the final score 4-4.
