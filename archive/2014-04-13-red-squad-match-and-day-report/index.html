Red Squad Match and Day Report

Sun Apr 13 2014, by Sam and Liam

Today we faced St.Pauls under 12 who are one of Jersey’s top teams. Stanly scored a team goal midway in the second half, and after that Charlie made a brilliant save and we held on for a 1-0 win. It wasn’t just Charlie who played well throughout the game, there were other people such as Fred, Harry, Stanly, Ollie L , Oli J,Liam,Sam,Adam,James and Max. Later on we went to the beach and James chased a ball into a waterfall twice! Dinner was a four course meal that included starters,soup,main course and pudding.
