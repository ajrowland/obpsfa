Reds report

Mon Apr 10 2017, by Alex, Joe and Ellis

⟵ Back to archive

To start the day off, we went to breakfast at 8:30 and we all enjoyed engulfing a lovely full English. After that, we packed our bags ready for a big day because we played Jersey year-fives academy team. We had a bit of a mix around and played in unusual positions. Our keeper,Joseph, who played left midfield scored the winning goal to end the match 2-1 and made our win to loss ratio 2/0 Joe Rowland played in goal and almost kept a clean sheet. Next along the line, we all went to fun exciting Aqua Splash, which is a water park. We played tag in the wave machine and the outside pool. We also went down the flumes. Most of us had pork steak. We hope to win tomorrow.
