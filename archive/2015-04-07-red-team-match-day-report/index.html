Red Team Match / Day Report

Tue Apr 07 2015, by Georgie Belchamber and Daniel Jones

⟵ Back to archive

On Tuesday 7th April Orpington B played Potters Bar A. Unfortunately we lost 4-0 but we didn’t stop trying and pot 100% effort in and just could not squeeze a goal in. After the game against Potters Bar we had mild chicken curry and apple crumble for lunch. 30 minutes later Orpington went to Aqua Splash. Aqua Splash is a very enjoyable water park. After having fun at Aqua Splash, we took a walk down to Costcutter to buy some sweets for our hotel.

For our dinner we had Duck breast and fudge cake. At 9 o’clock some of us went to the beach, we ran past a speed check and the majority of us got 16 miles per hour. On our journey back we was all really tired, when we reached the hotel we put our head on the pillow and shut our eyes.
