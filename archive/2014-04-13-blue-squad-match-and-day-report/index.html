Blue Squad Match and Day Report

Sun Apr 13 2014, by Emanuel and Liam

⟵ Back to archive

Our very first game was against Potters Bar. We got off to a great start with having most of the possession, from a great pass from Jude, but unfortunately we didn’t take our chances with Archie missing from close range and Cameron scuffing the shot and it went straight at the keeper. Adam made an amazing double save witch kept us in the game and then that led to half time with the score at 0-0.

As the second half got on the way Dom got us a free kick in a very dangerous position witch Liam took but just skimmed the outside of the post and went wide. With ten minutes to go Archie put in a super ball to Cameron and Cameron finished it beautifully. Soon after they scored a scruffy goal due to a number of players losing concentration this then bought the game to an end with the score being 1-1.

After the game we went to Greve De Lecqe beach. We all had a great time running in the sea and playing on the sand. After a while we went to the shop and bought ice cream, drinks, and sweets. Then we went back to our hotel and had are evening meal.
