Clubs rally to our call

We are delighted to announce our three teams (Blues Boys, Reds Boys & Girls) will be playing our home games at Farnborough Old Boys Guild Football Club, Petts Wood Football Club & FC Elmstead Football Club this season. All three clubs have been amazing and we see long partnerships with them to create a platform for boys & girls to play football and develop as players.

We were in an unfortunate position earlier in the year where Orpington Rovers parted ways with us. Through the support of a number of individuals, grassroots clubs, Schools, supporters & ex Orpington players spreading the word of our search for a new home. We were able to secure this. It had an incredible response with a number of offers from Grassroots clubs and we want to take a moment to thank everyone for their support. Schools football & District football is such a special experience so we are thank to be able to offer this opportunity to boys & girls when it is safe for football to return.
