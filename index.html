Orpington and District PSFA

We are the Orpington and District Primary Schools Football Association. Giving the opportunity for boys in Primary school to play football at the highest level. The representative teams of Orpington and District PSFA receive no external funding and rely entirely on the support of parents and friends at our various fundraising events.
